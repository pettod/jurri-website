Background

JURRI is the judo club of Tampere University Hervanta campus (TUNI).

Training time and place

On Mondays at 6:00pm to 7:30pm
On Wednesdays at 6:00pm to 7:30pm
Training place is Bombshelter (in Finnish Bommari)

Bombshelter's tatami

Tatami has been reconstructed in 2010 and can be found from downstairs in Rakennustalo behind the floorball courts. Pass through the red door. If necessary, the space between the orange lockers shown in the picture serves as a changing room. Remember to stamp your access card on the card reader on the right side of the elevator!
