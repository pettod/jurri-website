PRICE LIST

Costs of training

Prices

Membership fee5 € per year
Training fee17 € per season
(1st of Sep to 31st of Aug)
SportUni fee75 € per year
Judo Federation membership fee78 € per year (voluntary)

The money covers first aid costs, recompense for coaches, grading and sauna evenings. SportUni fee must be paid in order to be a member of JURRI. This fee gives you access to the sports services of Tampere Universities. The membership fee of the Finnish Judo Federation is voluntary and covers the insurance premium. You cannot participate in belt graduations or Federation's events without the Federation's membership.

You can find cheap budo suits from the Näähalli's bullet boards or from the Internet. A new judo suit + belt cost about 100 to 200 €.
