JURRI's background

Activities

JURRI (in Finnish JUdoa haRRastavat teekkarIt) means technology students who practice judo. JURRI operates under TUrVoKe (Tampere University’s sports club). JURRI is TUNI’s Judo Club. Its goal is to promote judo activity in TUNI and offer judo teaching for reasonable prices for students of TUNI.

History

JURRI was founded in autumn 2005. JURRI has its own board whose members will take care of needed equipment and organizing events. JURRI's board will be chosen every autumn meeting.
